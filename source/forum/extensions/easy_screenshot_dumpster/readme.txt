##        Mod title:  Easy Screenshot Dumpster
##
##      Mod version:  1.2.1
##   Works on PunBB:  1.3.2
##     Release date:  2009-02-26
##           Author:  CrustyDOD
##
##      Description:  Mod that integrates image hosting into your PunBB forum. 
##					  Uploaded images are not hosted on your forum, but on remote 
##					  image hosting website screenshotdumpster.com. When user uploads images, the 
##					  BBCode for thumbnails is automatically created and inserted into the post the 
##					  user is typing.
##					  It's that easy!
##
##   Affected files:  post.php
##                    viewtopic.php
##					  lang/post.php
##
##       Affects DB:  No
##
##            Notes:  
##
##       DISCLAIMER:  Please note that "mods" are not officially supported by
##                    PunBB. Installation of this modification is done at your
##                    own risk. Backup your forum database and any and all
##                    applicable files before proceeding.
